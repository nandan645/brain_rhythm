Surobane

Study to understand rhythmic organization in
biology and applied neuro-engineering
(Surobane)

Electrical, magnetic and mechanical vortices are generated by secondary structures of proteins to large organs. The map of biological rhythms should not be limited to ionic rhythms as it is today. We need to map biorhythms in the entire electromagnetic spectrum.

There have been several efforts in the last thousands of years to map biorhythms. We would organize series of meetings “Surobane” to bring researchers working on compiling biological rhythms and associated neuroscience all over the world.
